Our Mission

To create an open platform that democratizes access to quality educational materials, fostering collaboration and knowledge sharing among students.

How It Works

Students and faculty can upload their notes, presentations, and other study materials which are then organized and made available to everyone.

Community Guidelines

We encourage respectful sharing of materials while maintaining academic integrity.

  • Only share materials you have permission to distribute
  • Clearly label and categorize your uploads
  • Respect copyright and intellectual property
